OKIRA PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS DOCUMENT 
(PRD) 
1. Overview 
Product Name: Okira 
Purpose: To help Nigerian businesses automate lead generation and outreach 
using AI-powered personalization. 
Goal: Reduce time wasted on low-quality outreach by automatically 
discovering, verifying, and engaging leads with context-aware messaging. 
Target Users: 
● Sales teams within tech companies 
● Marketing agencies managing multiple clients 
● Individual marketing professionals and founders seeking leads 
2. Problem Statement 
Businesses and marketers in Nigeria spend hours searching for leads manually, 
verifying contact information, and crafting outreach messages that often feel 
generic. This leads to low response rates and inefficient use of sales and 
marketing time. 
3. Solution 
Okira automates the entire prospecting pipeline: 
1. Lead Discovery: Finds potential leads using LinkedIn and Google My 
Business. 
2. Lead Verification: Validates emails and phone numbers for accuracy. 
 
3. AI Personalization: Generates tailored outreach messages designed to 
increase reply rates. 
 
4. Export & Integration: Allows export to CSV or integration with CRM 
systems (in later versions).

6. Target Market 
● Primary: Nigerian B2B companies, marketing agencies, and tech startups 
● Expansion (3-year goal): Broader African market (Ghana, Kenya, South

7. User Persona (MVP Focus) 
Name: Tunde, Sales Manager at a Tech Company 
● Goal: Generate new B2B leads weekly for his company’s SaaS product 
● Frustration: Spends hours filtering LinkedIn profiles manually 
● Okira helps by: Automating discovery and sending pre-personalized 
outreach templates 
8. User Flow (Basic MVP Flow) 
1. User logs in / signs up 
2. Input search parameters: keywords, industry, location 
3. Okira scrapes and lists leads 
4. Leads are verified automatically (email/phone) 
5. AI generates a custom message for each lead 
6. User reviews results and exports list to CSV
